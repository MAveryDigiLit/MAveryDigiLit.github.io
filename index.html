About

Hello! My name’s Matt Avery and I’ve got two major hobbies: sculpting with cardboard and playing the trumpet. I’ve used cardboard to sculpt a horse and buggy, cow head, camel in camo, and many more unexpected objects. I started playing trumpet in 4th grade and have since performed around the world. I hope to one day incorporate these two hobbies into my career.
